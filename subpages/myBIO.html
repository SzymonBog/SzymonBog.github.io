I don't expect anyone to know/remember me. Even if you think that you know/remember me you probably don't know the real me. I've changed a lot since 2017 and I didn't want to show it. I think that I was afraid to be rejected. But let's start from the begining.

Childhood

To be honest I can barely remember my childhood, maybe because nothing special happened at that time or maybe because my bad memories replaced the good ones or because my childhood ended when I was 14 years old. The only things I remember from those times are certain events with friends, including one school trip in 6-th grade. That's it. When I think about this, it's kinda sad.
